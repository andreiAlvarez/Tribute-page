Here's a time line of Linus Torvalds's life:

    Dec 28, 1969, Born into the world.

    1988-1996, Studied at the University of Helsinki.

    1991, Obtained MINIX OS, Version 0.01, GNU + Linux Kernal.

    Jan 1, 1996, Started Work at Tansmeta.

    May 1, 1996, Tux is suggested.

    Sep 1, 1996, Linux Trademark Disputes.

    Aug 1, 1998, Linus makes the cover of Forbes.

    Jan 1, 1999, offered stock options by Red Hat.

    May 1, 2001, Just for Fun released.

    Feb 1, 2002, Bitkeeper causes controversy.

    Jun 1, 2004, moved to Oregon.

    Apr 7, 2005, creates "Git".

    Jan 1, 2006, cumulatively contributed 2% of the Linux.

    Jan 1, 2006, Torvalds does not support GNU v3.

    2012, Torvalds was inducted into the Internet Hall of Fame.
